The Corona Situation by jiatao on Sketchfab

Name: The Corona Situation
Medium: Meshmixer




  This 3D sculpture was made in the start of the COVID-19 lockdown. It was created to represent my experience of the lockdown. The center-piece of the sculpture was inspired by the popular internet "This is Fine" meme, which depict an apathic anthropomorphic dog sitting surround by the fire (https://knowyourmeme.com/memes/this-is-fine). During that time, I shared the same sentiment as the dog depicted in the meme: feeling that the world is ending.
